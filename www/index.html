Quick Patient Counseling

About

For busy health professionals (pharmacists, doctors, nurses and others) who intend to counsel their patients on medication use, Quick Patient Counseling (QPC) App provides CONCISE, and RELEVANT drug information in a quick-search, single view and non-verbose manner.

Network connection is NOT required except when loading database first time after installation or whenever you update data.

How to use
Start typing drug name (brand or generic) and touch it in the results list that appears and you will see the following drug info in one-view. It’s that simple and QUICK!
  1. Take with or without food?
  2. Sedation Risk
  3. Pregnancy and Lactation Safety
  4. Major Side Effects (SEs)
  5. Caution and Contraindication (XI)
  6. Black Box Warning (BBW), if any
  7. Key Counseling Points
  8. User Notes
Key Counseling Points have been divided into:
  1. Keywords: Keywords are main/common counseling points and appear as Red Robin color buttons. If not sure what a certain keyword is referring to, just touch that button and definition/description will pop up (and disappear when touch is ended). This feature simplifies visual scanning of the info. In a spare time, user can browse through the list of keywords (included below TERMS AND CONDITIONS section) to develop and enhance familiarity.
  2. Text: Further info is presented as a short text. Wherever possible, full words and complete sentences are avoided – again to facilitate QUICK review. Abbreviation used are obvious and common. However, to avoid any confusion, a record in drug list is included with the name “Abbreviation Lists”, which contains all abbreviations used in this QPC App.
